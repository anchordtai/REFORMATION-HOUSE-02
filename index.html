Mission Statement

We are called with the mandate to preach globally the kingdom of God; righteousness through faith in the atoning work of the risen savior who will one day return and rule over the whole world.

We are called to reveal the mysteries, the victorious revelation knowledge of God's word, hidden from ages Col 1:25-28

We are called with the mandade of Evangelism and Mission in accordance with the christian commission (Matt. 28:19)

We are called to disciple believers around the World and prepare them for the Kingdom Of God.

Vision Statement

To become a global ministries, in dissolvable, Unmoveable Army of Jesus Christ.

To establish and plant our branches, around the world with the mandate to preach the Kingdom of God; righteousness through the faith in the atoning work of the risen saviour.

Faith Statement

WE BELIEVE in One God who is triune in nature- Father, Son and Holy Spirit, creator of all things.

WE BELIEVE in Jesus Christ the only begotten Son of God, was concieve of the Holy Spirit, born of the virgin Mary, was cricified, died, buried and He resurrected, and ascended into heaven and is sitted at the right hand of God the Father and is a true God and true man.

WE BELIEVE the entirety of the Bible as the only Holy Spirit inspired word of God and the infallible rule of faith and conduct

WE BELIEVE in the resurrection of the dead, the eternal happiness of the saved and the eternal purnishment of the lost.

WE BELIEVE in personal salvation of the believers through the shed blood of Jesus Christ.

WE BELIEVE in the sanctification through the word of God and by the Holy Spirit, and we believe in personal holiness, purity of heart and life.

WE BELIEVE in water baptism, baptism of the Holy Spirit as distinct from new birth in speaking in tongues as the Holy Spirit give utterance (Act 2:4).
