Who We Are

Welcome to Christ's Reformation House International. We are a vibrant, Christ-centered community dedicated to spiritual growth, outreach, and transformation. Our mission is to help you grow in your faith and connect with others, unveiling the mysteries of God's Kingdom through the revelation knowledge of God's word.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything Jesus commanded.

We strive to create a welcoming and nurturing environment where individuals can grow in faith, find community, and serve others with love and compassion.

Our Vision

To be a global beacon of hope, faith, and transformation, raising disciples who impact their world for Christ.

Our Faith Statement

We believe in the authority of the Bible, the power of prayer, and the necessity of a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. Our faith is rooted in love, grace, and the pursuit of holiness.

Our Community

We are a diverse, welcoming family united by faith. Join us for worship, fellowship, and service as we grow together in Christ.
