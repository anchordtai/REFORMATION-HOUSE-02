Daily Devotional

Today's Bible Reading

John 3:16 - "For God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life."

Devotional Message

God's love for us is immense and unconditional. Today's verse reminds us of the greatest gift we have received through Jesus Christ. Reflect on this love and let it guide your actions and thoughts throughout the day.

Prayer

Dear Lord, thank you for your unconditional love and for the gift of your Son, Jesus Christ. Help me to reflect this love in my life and share it with others. Amen.

Additional Reflection

Take some time today to meditate on God's word and how it applies to your life. Let His guidance direct your path and decisions. Share this message with someone who might need encouragement.
